List of allowed functions and characters

  • All numbers (including the decimal point, when used appropriately)
  • t (the parameter variable)
  • Parenthesis (make sure they match!)
  • +, -, *, /, ^
  • cos, sin, tan, cot, sec, csc
  • arccos, arcsin, arctan
  • sqrt
  • log, ln, logbase{base}
  • pi, e

Use brackets {} after each function. Sine is sin{}, square root is sqrt{}, and logarithm is logbase{base}{}.


Sample valid entries:

  • sin{3*t}
  • sqrt{logbase{2}{t}}
  • 3*(e+2)*t
  • arctan{pi*((t-17)^{2})}
